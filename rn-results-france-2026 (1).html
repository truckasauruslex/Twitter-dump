Analysis

The trend lines below reveal a moderate positive correlation: RN tends to perform better in smaller, lower-ranked cities. The very largest French cities — Paris, Lyon, Toulouse — are clearly more resistant to the far right. Perpignan, Toulon, Nice, and Marseille are the outliers pulling the trend upward.
